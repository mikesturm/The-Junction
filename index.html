Welcome to The Junction

The Junction is a podcast about Medium--the well-known social network for writers and readers alike. Every two weeks, your hosts reach out and touch each other across the Atlantic Ocean, and talk about the writing they've stumbled across on Medium--good, bad, and ugly. They also allow ample time to muse on the deeper issues of life, writing, the internet, and whatever else comes up.
